Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Your financial data is private and belongs to you. We do not sell it, share it with advertisers, or use it to target you with ads.

1. Who We Are

Fintrail Pro is a personal finance web application available at fintrail.app. We help users track budgets, manage bills, set savings goals, and improve their financial health.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Your name and email address
  • Financial data: Transactions, bills, savings goals, and debt information you enter manually
  • Usage data: Features used, streaks, and XP progress
  • Payment information: Processed securely by Stripe — we never see or store your card number
  • Receipt uploads: Images you upload to verify bill payments or savings deposits

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Fintrail app
  • To process your Pro subscription via Stripe
  • To send important account notifications
  • To power the AI Financial Advisor (your messages are sent to Anthropic's API — not stored or used for training)
  • To calculate your financial health score and gamification progress

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase with row-level security so only you can access your own data. All data is encrypted in transit via HTTPS.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — Database and authentication
  • Stripe — Payment processing (PCI-compliant)
  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI Financial Advisor
  • Netlify — Web hosting

6. Your Rights

  • Access all data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data via your Profile settings
  • Delete your account and all data at any time
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

7. Children's Privacy

Fintrail Pro is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
